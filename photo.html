Retail:

AR applications allow customers to visualize products in their own space before making a purchase. For instance, furniture retailers use AR to let customers see how a piece of furniture would look in their home.

Real Estate:

AR is utilized in property listings to provide virtual tours of homes, allowing potential buyers to explore properties remotely and visualize the space.
